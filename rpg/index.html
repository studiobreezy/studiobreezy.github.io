Hidden Peak: A Tale From Highspire

A cozy adventure and farming RPG set in a former ghost town high in the mountains. As players settle into life in Highspire, they’ll farm, explore, help rebuild the community, and uncover mysteries - including the remnants of an unusual craft that may not be from this world.

In Development

Completed Systems & Features

  • Dialogue System — typing effect, speaker tags, player movement freeze
  • Inventory — stackable items, tooltips, use/drop functions, protected items
  • Day/Night Cycle — dynamic lighting synced with the game clock
  • Scene Transitions — with spawn points, scene fading, and additive loading
  • NPC Daily Schedules — pathfinding, persistent positioning, branching dialogue
  • Currency System — coins
  • Farming System — plant, grow, and harvest seasonal crops
  • Shop System — interactable NPC shopkeeper, purchase items from shop counters and shelves, rotating seed display
  • Save System — player progress, time of day, inventory, and more
  • Quest System — track and manage player quests
  • Seasonal Tilemaps — dynamically changing environments based on the season

Currently Working On

  • Turn-Based Battles — strategic combat system with player and enemy turns, status effects, use items and special abilities
  • Character Creation — customize your character's appearance and attributes
  • Weather System — dynamic seasonal weather effects
  • Placeable Furniture — place, rotate, and pick up furniture items
  • Camping system — placeable tents and campfires from inventory
  • In-Game Desktop — a lore delivery system with access to various minimal in-game applications
  • Mining — gather resources from a vast cave in Highspire
  • Crafting System — create items and tools from gathered resources in the player's workshop
  • Intro Story — intro cutscene and player waking in the town Inn
  • Worldbuilding — interior scenes (arcade, homes, shops, caves, and more), exterior locations to explore, Highspire’s lore and history
  • Emotional story arcs tied to exploration, memory, and community
  • More NPC characters — diverse personalities and roles within the game world
  • Improved Quest system — more complex and branching quests with multiple outcomes
  • Mini-Game Integration — several playable arcade games
  • Arcade Prize System — earn and redeem prizes from arcade games
  • Improved Game Audio — improved sound effects and background music

Prototyping & Future Goals

  • Life other than NPCs and plant life — introduce animals, wildlife, and other interactive elements
  • Calendar-based events — dynamic events and activities driven by the in-game calendar
  • Upgradable player cabin — enhance, expand, and customize the player's cabin over time
  • Accessibility improvements — adjustable text speed, UI scaling, control remapping using Unity's new Input System
  • Ongoing polish and performance improvements — optimization, visual refinement, and UX enhancements
