About Me

Hi! I'm Wendy — a game developer, tools creator, and pixel artist behind the persona CrazyBirdLady. I'm passionate about building 2D worlds, scripting gameplay systems, and creating polished player experiences. I love combining code, art, and storytelling, and I'm currently developing a cozy adventure RPG.
