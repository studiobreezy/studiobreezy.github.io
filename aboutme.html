About Me

Hi! I'm Wendy — game developer, tools creator, and pixel artist behind the persona CrazyBirdLady. I specialize in 2D Unity development, gameplay systems, and building cozy pixel art worlds.

Lately I’ve been working on systems for weather, turn-based battles, and furniture placement in my current RPG project.

Combining code, art, and storytelling has become my favorite kind of “core gameplay loop” — as a human.

I publish assets on Itch.io, maintain an editor tool on the Unity Asset Store, and continue expanding my skills through courses and self-directed learning.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually hanging out with my parakeets or tinkering with new ideas for my game world.
